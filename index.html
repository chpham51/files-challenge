Amelie Poulain


The only thing bigger than Amelie's imagination is her heart. Whimsical, clever, and dreamy, Amelie is a shy Parisian waitress. After a surprise discovery, she makes it her mission to help others which helps distract her from her own problems. Yet what happens when in the process, she finds love? Amelie and her charming adventures are sure to bring a smile to your face.
Le fabuleux destin d' Amelie Poulain
